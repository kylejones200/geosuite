About Strip Charts

Strip charts (also called log plots or well log displays) are the traditional method for visualizing well log data in the oil and gas industry. Each vertical track represents a different measurement:

  • GR (Gamma Ray): Natural radioactivity of formations
  • RHOB (Bulk Density): Formation bulk density
  • NPHI (Neutron Porosity): Hydrogen index/porosity
  • RT (Resistivity): Formation electrical resistivity
  • PE (Photoelectric): Lithology indicator
  • DT (Sonic): Acoustic travel time

Tips: Use your mouse to zoom and pan. Hover over traces to see values. The depth axis increases downward, following industry convention.
